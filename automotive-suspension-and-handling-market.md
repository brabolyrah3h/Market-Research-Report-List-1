<p><p>The automotive suspension and handling market encompasses various types including control arms, solid/live axles, multilink systems, and others. Control arms facilitate wheel movement and alignment, enhancing stability and ride comfort. Solid or live axles provide durability and load-bearing capacity, often used in trucks and off-road vehicles.
