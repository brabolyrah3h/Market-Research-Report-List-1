<p><strong>The Viral Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Fluorescent Antibody (DFA) Test</li><li>Immunochromatographic Assay</li><li>RT-PCR-Based Test</li><li>Agglutination Assay</li><li>Flow-Through Assay</li><li>Solid-Phase Assay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The viral testing market encompasses various diagnostic methods. Direct Fluorescent Antibody (DFA) tests use fluorescent tags to detect viral antigens in samples. Immunochromatographic Assays utilize a simple lateral flow method for rapid results. RT-PCR-Based Tests amplify viral RNA for accurate detection. Agglutination Assays involve the clumping of particles in the presence of a virus. Flow-Through Assays use a membrane for result visualization, while Solid-Phase Assays employ a solid support to capture and detect viral components effectively.</p></p>
