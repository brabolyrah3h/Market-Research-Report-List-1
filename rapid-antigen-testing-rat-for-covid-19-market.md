<p><p>Rapid Antigen Testing (RAT) for COVID-19 is categorized into several market types based on the detection methods used. Fluorescence Immunochromatography utilizes fluorescent markers for enhanced sensitivity and rapid results. Colloidal Gold Immunochromatography, the most common type, employs gold nanoparticles for visual detection of antigens. Latex Immunochromatography uses latex particles to capture antibodies, providing quick results while maintaining specificity. "Others" encompasses alternative testing methods or emerging technologies aimed at improving accuracy and convenience in antigen detection for COVID-19.</p></p>
