<p><ul><li>Endoparasiticides</li><li>Ectoparasiticides</li><li>Endectocides</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The animal insecticide market is categorized into three main types: Endoparasiticides, Ectoparasiticides, and Endectocides. Endoparasiticides target internal parasites such as worms and protozoa, providing treatment for livestock and pets. Ectoparasiticides focus on external parasites like fleas, ticks, and mites, commonly used in companion animals and livestock. Endectocides combine the effects of both, effectively addressing internal and external parasites simultaneously. These classifications are essential for tailored pest management strategies in agriculture and veterinary practices.</p></p>
