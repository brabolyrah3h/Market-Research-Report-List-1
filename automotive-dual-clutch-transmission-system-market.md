<p><p>The automotive dual clutch transmission (DCT) system market features several types of clutches: Wet Multiplate Clutches, Dry Single-plate Clutches, and Others. Wet Multiplate Clutches utilize multiple friction plates submerged in oil, offering enhanced cooling and performance, making them suitable for high-torque applications. Dry Single-plate Clutches are simpler, lighter, and more cost-effective, ideal for smaller vehicles. Other variants may include innovative designs or hybrid technologies, catering to diverse automotive needs and preferences across various vehicle segments.</p></p>
