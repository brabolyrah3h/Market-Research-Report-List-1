<p><p>Band ligators for hemorrhoid treatment are categorized into two main types: endoscopic ligators and non-endoscopic ligators. Endoscopic ligators are designed for use during endoscopic procedures, allowing for precise band application under direct visualization, enhancing accuracy and patient comfort. Non-endoscopic ligators, on the other hand, are used in a more straightforward manner, often in outpatient settings or less invasive procedures. Both types aim to effectively treat hemorrhoids by cutting off blood flow, causing the tissue to shrink and heal.</p></p>
