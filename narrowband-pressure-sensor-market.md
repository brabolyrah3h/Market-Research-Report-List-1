<p><strong>The Narrowband Pressure Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capacitive</li><li>Piezoresistive</li><li>Piezoelectric</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Narrowband Pressure Sensor market is primarily segmented into four types: </p><p>1. **Capacitive** sensors measure pressure by detecting changes in capacitance, offering high sensitivity and stability.</p><p>2. **Piezoresistive** sensors utilize the change in electrical resistance when pressure is applied, providing accurate readings in various applications.</p><p>3. **Piezoelectric** sensors generate an electrical charge in response to applied pressure, excelling in dynamic pressure measurements.</p><p>4. **Others** encompass specialized technologies, like optical or microelectromechanical systems (MEMS), catering to niche applications within pressure sensing.</p></p>
