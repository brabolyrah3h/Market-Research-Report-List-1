<p><ul><li>Mechanical Cooling Fan Control Module</li><li>Electric Cooling Fan Control Module</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Cooling Fan Control Module market is divided into two main types: Mechanical Cooling Fan Control Modules and Electric Cooling Fan Control Modules. Mechanical Cooling Fan Control Modules utilize a viscous coupling mechanism to drive the fan based on engine temperature, providing reliability and simplicity. In contrast, Electric Cooling Fan Control Modules use electric motors for operation, allowing for precise control, lower emissions, and improved fuel efficiency. Both types play critical roles in regulating engine temperature and optimizing performance.</p></p>
