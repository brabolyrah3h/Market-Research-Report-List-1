<p><p>The agricultural dyestuff market can be categorized into two main types: solution and powder. Solution dyestuffs are typically liquid forms that facilitate easy application and absorption, making them ideal for use in various agricultural practices such as dyeing textiles or coloring products. In contrast, powder dyestuffs are solid forms that require mixing with water or other solvents before application. They offer longer shelf life and can be more economical for larger-scale operations, catering to diverse agricultural needs.</p></p>
