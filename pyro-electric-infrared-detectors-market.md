Single Channel Detectors focus on detecting temperature changes from one infrared source, making them suitable for simple applications. In contrast, Multi-Channel Detectors can monitor multiple infrared sources simultaneously, allowing for more complex analysis and improved performance in advanced applications like surveillance and industrial processes. The choice between them depends on the specific requirements of sensitivity, response time, and application complexity.</p></p>
