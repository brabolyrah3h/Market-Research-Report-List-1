<p><p>The automotive additive manufacturing market includes various processes such as Powder Bed Fusion, which uses laser or electron beams to fuse powdered materials, and Binder Jetting, where a liquid binder is used to bond particles. Directed Energy Deposition involves melting materials as they are deposited. Material Extrusion shapes thermoplastic filament through a nozzle, while Material Jetting sprays droplets of material for layer-by-layer creation. Sheet Lamination involves stacking and bonding sheets, while Vat Polymerization uses light to cure liquid resins into solid parts.</p></p>
