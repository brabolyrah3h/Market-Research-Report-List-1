<p><p>The 3D sensing technology market includes several key types. Stereoscopic vision uses two cameras to capture depth information, enabling reliable depth perception. Structured light pattern employs a projector to cast a known light pattern onto a scene, which software interprets to create a 3D model. Time of flight measures the time it takes for light to bounce back from objects, providing precise distance measurements. Ultrasound utilizes sound waves for depth detection, while other methods encompass various emerging technologies in 3D sensing.</p></p>
