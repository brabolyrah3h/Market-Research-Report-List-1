<p><p>Sewage treatment facilities utilize various technologies to enhance wastewater management. Moving Bed Biofilm Reactors (MBBR) employ a combination of biofilm and suspended growth processes to effectively degrade organic matter. Ozone-Based Advanced Oxidation utilizes ozone to break down pollutants, improving water quality through powerful oxidation. Powdered Activated Carbon (PAC) integrates with traditional treatment systems to adsorb contaminants and enhance removal efficiency. These methods are vital in addressing environmental concerns and ensuring clean water supply through efficient and innovative sewage treatment solutions.</p></p>
