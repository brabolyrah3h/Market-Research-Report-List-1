<p><strong>The MEMS-Based Resonators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Series Resonance Type</li><li>Parallel Resonance Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>MEMS-based resonators are classified primarily into two market types: series resonance and parallel resonance. </p><p>Series resonance types operate when the resonant frequency results in maximum current flow, making them ideal for applications needing high selectivity, such as filters in communication devices. </p><p>Parallel resonance types, conversely, resonate at a frequency that allows maximum voltage across the device, favoring applications like oscillators and sensors. Both types leverage micro-electromechanical systems technology for compactness and efficiency, catering to diverse electronic sectors.</p></p>
