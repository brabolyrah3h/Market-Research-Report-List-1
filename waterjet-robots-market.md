<p><ul><li>Continuous Jet</li><li>Pulse Jet</li><li>Cavitation Jet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The waterjet robots market is segmented into three main types: continuous jet, pulse jet, and cavitation jet. Continuous jet systems deliver a steady stream of water, ideal for precision cutting and machining tasks. Pulse jet systems utilize rapid bursts of water to enhance cutting efficiency and minimize material waste. Cavitation jet technology generates high-energy bubbles that implode on the material surface, offering unique advantages in cleaning and surface preparation. Each type caters to specific industrial applications, emphasizing precision and effectiveness.</p></p>
