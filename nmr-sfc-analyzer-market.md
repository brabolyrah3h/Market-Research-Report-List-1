<p><p>The NMR SFC (Nuclear Magnetic Resonance Surface Functionalization Characterization) Analyzer market is categorized into two main types: Time Domain and Frequency Domain. Time Domain NMR analyzes signals over time to reveal molecular dynamics and interactions, offering high-resolution insights into sample behavior. In contrast, Frequency Domain NMR focuses on spectral information, providing valuable data on molecular structure and composition by measuring frequency shifts. Both approaches serve distinct applications in research and industry, enhancing material characterization and analysis capabilities.</p></p>
