<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Security CMOS Image Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>FSI</li><li>BSI</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Security CMOS Image Sensor market is primarily categorized into two types: Front-Side Illuminated (FSI) and Back-Side Illuminated (BSI) sensors. FSI sensors capture light from the surface, making them suitable for well-lit environments but less effective in low light. In contrast, BSI sensors have their circuitry behind the light-sensitive area, enhancing light capture and performance in low-light conditions, improving image quality in surveillance applications. This differentiation caters to varying security needs across diverse environments.</p></p>
