<p><strong>The SC Fiber Connector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Single-mode"</li><li>"Multi-mode"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The SC fiber connector market is divided into two primary types: single-mode and multi-mode. Single-mode connectors are designed for long-distance communication, using a single light pathway to minimize signal loss and support higher bandwidths over greater distances. In contrast, multi-mode connectors are suited for shorter distances, utilizing multiple light paths to transmit data simultaneously, which is effective in local area networks. Both types cater to specific networking needs, influencing their adoption in various telecommunications applications.</p></p>
