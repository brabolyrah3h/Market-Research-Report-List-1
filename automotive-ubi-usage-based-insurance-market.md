<p><p>The automotive usage-based insurance (UBI) market consists of various types based on data collection methods. Odometer data-based UBI relies on vehicle mileage to assess risk and adjust premiums accordingly. GPS data-based UBI uses real-time location and driving behavior data, including speed, acceleration, and braking patterns, to provide a more dynamic risk assessment. Other market types may include telematics devices that combine both odometer and GPS data, as well as smartphone apps that track driving habits to personalize insurance offerings.</p></p>
