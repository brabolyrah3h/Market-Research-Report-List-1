<p><p>Deep Brain Stimulation (DBS) devices are classified into single-channel and dual-channel types, each serving different clinical needs. Single-channel DBS devices deliver stimulation through one electrode, targeting a specific brain region, often used in simpler cases like essential tremor. Dual-channel DBS devices, on the other hand, can stimulate multiple areas simultaneously, allowing for more comprehensive treatment of complex conditions such as Parkinson's disease. This versatility makes dual-channel devices increasingly popular for nuanced therapeutic approaches in neurology.</p></p>
