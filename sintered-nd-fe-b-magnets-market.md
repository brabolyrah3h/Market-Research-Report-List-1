<p><ul><li>Axially Magnetized</li><li>Diametrically Magnetized</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sintered Nd-Fe-B magnets market is primarily categorized into axially magnetized and diametrically magnetized types. Axially magnetized magnets have magnetic poles on their flat surfaces, making them ideal for applications requiring lengthwise magnetic fields, such as motors and generators. Conversely, diametrically magnetized magnets possess magnetic poles along their diameter, enabling efficient use in cylindrical applications and providing effective torque in devices like sensors and actuators. Each type caters to specific industrial needs, influencing their market demand and applications.</p></p>
