<p><strong>The Semiconductor Photomask Inspection Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Die to Die (DD) Method</li><li>Die to Database (DB) Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor photomask inspection equipment market features two main methodologies: Die to Die (DD) and Die to Database (DB). The DD method involves comparing features on one die to those on another die from the same wafer, enabling direct detection of defects and ensuring uniformity across devices. Conversely, the DB method compares die features against a predefined database of known correct patterns, facilitating identification of deviations and errors by matching the current die against established benchmarks. Both methods enhance quality control in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
