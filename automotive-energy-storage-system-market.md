<p><p>The Automotive Energy Storage System market comprises three primary types: flywheels, batteries, and supercapacitors. Flywheels store kinetic energy, offering rapid discharge capabilities and long lifecycle, ideal for applications requiring high power output. Batteries, particularly lithium-ion, provide substantial energy density for electric vehicles, enabling longer ranges. Supercapacitors excel in quick energy absorption and delivery, complementing batteries by managing power fluctuations. Together, these technologies contribute to energy efficiency and performance in modern automotive applications, addressing the growing demand for sustainable transportation.</p></p>
