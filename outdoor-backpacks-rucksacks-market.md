<p><p>The outdoor backpacks and rucksacks market consists of three main types: frameless backpacks, internal-frame backpacks, and external-frame backpacks. Frameless backpacks are lightweight and flexible, ideal for minimalist hikers. Internal-frame backpacks feature a built-in support structure that stabilizes the load and enhances comfort for longer treks. External-frame backpacks have a rigid frame outside the pack, offering improved ventilation and heavier load capacity, making them suitable for carrying bulky gear. Each type caters to different hiking styles and preferences.</p></p>
