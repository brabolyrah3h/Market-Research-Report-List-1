<p><strong>The C-SiC Composites Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Carbon Particles and Silicon Carbide (Cp/SiC) Composites</li><li>Carbon Fiber and Silicon Carbide (Cf/SiC) Composites</li></ul></p>
<p><p>C-SiC composites are advanced materials combining carbon with silicon carbide, offering exceptional thermal stability and strength. The Carbon Particles and Silicon Carbide (Cp/SiC) composites utilize carbon particles embedded in a silicon carbide matrix, enhancing mechanical properties and thermal conductivity. In contrast, Carbon Fiber and Silicon Carbide (Cf/SiC) composites integrate carbon fibers with silicon carbide, resulting in lightweight, high-performance materials suitable for aerospace and industrial applications. Both types leverage the advantageous properties of their components, catering to diverse market needs.</p></p>
